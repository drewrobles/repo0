`j` down

`k` up

`l` right

`w` left by a word

`b` right by a word

`dd` delete line

`u` undo

`^r` redo

`#[hjkl]` motion

`d#[hjklwb]` delete with motion

`i` insert mode left of character

`a` insert mode right of character

`v` visual mode
